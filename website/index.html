How it works

From signup to live in 4 steps

No complex setup required.

1
Create your account
Sign up with your company name and email. You'll get your Company ID and API Key instantly.
2
Open the admin dashboard
Log in to the Flutter dashboard app. Upload your FAQ, add intents, and train the bot – no coding needed.
3
Embed in one line
Add the embed script to your website using your API Key.
<script src="widget.js" data-key="YOUR_KEY"></script>
4
Go live
Customers get instant, accurate answers 24/7. Monitor everything from the dashboard.
