How it works

From signup to live in 4 steps

No complex setup required.

1
Create your account
Sign up with your company name. You'll receive your Company ID and API Key immediately — by email and in your dashboard.
2
Upload your knowledge base
Upload PDFs, text files, or FAQ docs. Our AI processes them — your bot now knows your business.
3
Embed in one line
Add the embed script to your website using the API Key, or use the Flutter SDK with your Company ID.
<script src="widget.js" data-key="YOUR_KEY"></script>
4
Go live
Customers get instant, accurate answers 24/7. Monitor everything from the dashboard.
